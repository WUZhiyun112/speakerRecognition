Project Mission

This project focuses on the practical deployment of speech technologies in unconstrained environments (e.g., hybrid meetings, smart offices). We aim to solve the core challenge of "Who Spoke What and When" by building a robust system that handles overlapping speech, background noise, and varying acoustic conditions.

Key Technological Pillars

  • Adaptive Feature Calibration: Integrating SE-Blocks to dynamically weigh acoustic channels, ensuring the model ignores "meeting room hum" and focuses on vocal clarity.
  • Context-Aware Speaker Embedding: Utilizing CAM++ architectures to capture fine-grained speaker identity even in short, rapid-fire dialogue.
  • On-device Efficiency: Optimizing the backbone with Depthwise Separable Convolutions to meet the low-latency requirements of real-time applications.
