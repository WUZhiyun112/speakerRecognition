# Application of Multi-speaker Dialogue Content Recognition Technology in Open Scenarios ![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Phase%202%20Active-orange) ![Category](https://img.shields.io/badge/Track-Application-red) ![Tech](https://img.shields.io/badge/Tech-PyTorch%20%7C%20ModelScope%20%7C%20VAD-blue) ## 🎯 Project Mission This project focuses on the practical deployment of speech technologies in **unconstrained environments** (e.g., hybrid meetings, smart offices). We aim to solve the **"Who Spoke What"** challenge by building a robust system that handles overlapping speech, background noise, and varying acoustic conditions. ## 🚀 Key Technological Pillars * **Adaptive Feature Calibration:** Integrating **SE-Blocks** to dynamically weigh acoustic channels, ensuring the model ignores "meeting room hum" and focuses on vocal clarity. * **Context-Aware Speaker Embedding:** Utilizing **CAM++** architectures to capture fine-grained speaker identity even in short, rapid-fire dialogue. * **On-device Efficiency:** Optimizing the backbone with **Depthwise Separable Convolutions** to meet the low-latency requirements of real-time application. --- ## 📈 Milestone Tracker ### ✅ Phase 1: Research & Infra (Completed) * **Survey:** Deep-dive into CAM++, Attention mechanisms, and lightweight CNNs. * **Environment:** Production-ready **PyTorch & ModelScope** setup on GPU clusters. * **Data Hub:** Unified pipeline for **VoxCeleb 1/2** and **CN-Celeb** datasets. ### 🏗️ Phase 2: Signal Engineering & Baseline (In-Progress) * **[Completed] Feature Pipeline:** Developed a high-fidelity extraction tool for **80-dim Fbank** features with custom windowing. * **[Completed] VAD System:** Debugged and deployed a specialized **Voice Activity Detection** module to strip environmental noise from open-scenario audio. * **[Current] Baseline Training:** Implementing the **CAM++ speaker verification baseline** to set our performance benchmark (EER/MinDCF). ### 🔮 Phase 3: System Optimization & Demo (Upcoming) * **SE-Block Integration:** Enhancing the frontend with channel-wise attention. * **Application Demo:** Developing a **"Live Voice-Lock"** and **"Speaker Diarization Timeline"** to visualize real-time separation of multiple speakers. --- ## 📂 Administrative Note > **Status:** Reclassification request to **"Application Track"** is currently pending with the Academic Affairs Office.
